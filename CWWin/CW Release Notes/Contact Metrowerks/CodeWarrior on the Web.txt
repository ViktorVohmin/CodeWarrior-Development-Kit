METROWERKS WORLDWIDE

Metrowerks is pleased to offer you a 24-hour-a-day, 365-day-per-year
information and support resource: Metrowerks Worldwide, available on
the World-Wide Web at

     http://www.metrowerks.com.

With Metrowerks Worldwide, you can:
        - ask technical support, sales, or registration questions
        - file bug reports or feature suggestions
        - download updates, patches, and new versions of tools
        - find user-contributed PowerPlant classes, tips, and techniques
        - find out more about Metrowerks, including an archive of press
           releases and financial results

Metrowerks Worldwide is available to anyone with an Internet
connection. This includes users of America Online, Prodigy, and
Compuserve who can use their online service's World-Wide Web browser.
